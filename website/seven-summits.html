The Seven Summits

🌏 Asia

珠穆朗玛峰 / Mount Everest

🇨🇳🇳🇵 China/Nepal · 8,849m

The highest point on Earth and the ultimate mountaineering challenge.

🌍 Europe

厄尔布鲁士峰 / Mount Elbrus

🇷🇺 Russia · 5,642m

The highest peak in Europe and the crown of the Caucasus mountains.

🌎 North America

迪纳利峰 / Denali

🇺🇸 USA (Alaska) · 6,190m

North America's highest peak, known for extreme cold and weather.

🌎 South America

阿空加瓜峰 / Aconcagua

🇦🇷 Argentina · 6,961m

The highest peak in the Western Hemisphere and a giant of the Andes.

🌍 Africa

乞力马扎罗峰 / Kilimanjaro

🇹🇿 Tanzania · 5,895m

Africa's highest peak, a dormant volcano and global adventure icon.

🌏 Oceania

查亚峰 / Puncak Jaya

🇮🇩 Indonesia · 4,884m

The highest island peak on Earth with remote technical approaches.

❄️ Antarctica

文森峰 / Vinson Massif

🇦🇶 Antarctica · 4,892m

The highest peak on the most remote and unforgiving continent.
